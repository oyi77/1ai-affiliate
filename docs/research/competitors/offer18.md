# Competitor: Offer18

- **URL:** https://offer18.com
- **Category:** direct
- **Core Value Prop:** Professional-grade performance marketing and affiliate tracking platform for networks, advertisers, and agencies. Strong automation, fraud detection, and white-labeling at a competitive price point.
- **Key Features:**
  1. S2S postback tracking with cookieless and probabilistic attribution
  2. Campaign automation — auto-pause/scale based on CTR, conversion rate, eCPM
  3. Built-in fraud detection for bot traffic and fraudulent conversions
  4. 75+ real-time data breakdowns (location, ISP, device, browser, traffic source)
  5. Full white-labeling — brand the platform as your own
  6. Customizable tracking links and granular user role management
  7. Native e-commerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce)
  8. Real-time click and conversion tracking
  9. Traffic distribution and offer management tools
  10. 14-day free trial
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Learning curve for users unfamiliar with advanced affiliate tracking concepts
  - Price increases over time reported by long-term users
  - Less brand recognition than Voluum or RedTrack — smaller community
  - Limited AI/ML-driven optimization features compared to premium competitors
- **Pricing Model:** Flat-rate monthly subscription starting at ~$49/mo. Tiers based on "units" (clicks + conversions). Contact sales for custom scaling. 14-day free trial available.
- **User Traction:** Strong presence on G2 and Capterra with positive reviews. Estimated 5K–15K active users. Popular among mid-sized affiliate networks and agencies in Asia and emerging markets.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Best-in-class white-labeling combined with high feature-to-cost ratio — allows networks to rebrand the entire platform as their own at a fraction of enterprise pricing.
- **What users complain about:**
  - Steep learning curve for beginners
  - Gradual price increases on existing plans
  - Smaller community and fewer third-party tutorials compared to Voluum/RedTrack
  - Interface can feel dated compared to newer SaaS trackers
