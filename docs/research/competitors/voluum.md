# Competitor: Voluum

- **URL:** https://voluum.com
- **Category:** direct
- **Core Value Prop:** Premium, enterprise-grade affiliate tracking platform for professional media buyers, high-volume affiliates, and agencies. Industry-leading AI-driven optimization and automation for large-scale paid advertising campaigns.
- **Key Features:**
  1. AI-driven traffic distribution — auto-routes traffic to most profitable offers/landers
  2. Automizer — rules engine to pause/scale campaigns across Facebook, Google, TikTok via API
  3. Anti-Fraud Kit — detects and blocks fraudulent traffic to protect ad budgets
  4. Granular multi-dimensional reporting — drill down by almost any variable
  5. In-panel guidance and test mode (2026) — test funnels without polluting reports
  6. Affilka integration (2025) — iGaming player reports pulled directly into Voluum
  7. Handles billions of events with enterprise-grade infrastructure
  8. Multi-user collaboration with role-based access
  9. Custom tracking domains and advanced redirect options
  10. Dedicated account management for enterprise tiers
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Highest entry price in the market — no free plan, starts at $149/mo
  - Strict no-refund policy — must commit carefully before subscribing
  - Overkill for beginners or low-volume affiliates
  - Complex feature set with steep learning curve
  - No self-hosting option — fully dependent on Voluum's cloud infrastructure
- **Pricing Model:** Tiered by event volume and features. No free plan. Annual billing available.
  - **Profit (Entry):** ~$149/mo — individual affiliates, basic AI features
  - **Scale/Business:** ~$299–500+/mo — higher limits, advanced automation
  - **Enterprise/Agency:** Custom — full features, dedicated support
- **User Traction:** Market leader for high-volume affiliate tracking. Estimated 50K–100K+ active users. Used by top media buying agencies and performance marketing firms globally. Strong brand recognition on STM Forum, AffiliateFix, and industry events.
- **Unique Differentiator:** AI-driven traffic optimization (Automizer) that automatically routes and manages campaigns across multiple ad networks — the most sophisticated automation engine in the affiliate tracking space.
- **What users complain about:**
  - Expensive — $149/mo minimum with no free tier
  - Strict no-refund policy frustrates users who need to cancel
  - Steep learning curve for the full feature set
  - Overkill for small affiliates — designed for $10K+/mo ad spend
  - Occasional reporting delays during high-traffic periods
  - Lock-in concerns — no self-hosting or data export alternatives
