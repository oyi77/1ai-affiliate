# Competitor: RedTrack

- **URL:** https://redtrack.io
- **Category:** direct
- **Core Value Prop:** All-in-one ad tracking and performance marketing platform that centralizes cross-channel data, provides server-side attribution, and automates campaign management. Built for professional media buyers and e-commerce brands.
- **Key Features:**
  1. Server-side tracking with first-party cookies — immune to ad blockers and iOS privacy restrictions
  2. Automation & rules engine — auto-pause/scale campaigns to protect profit margins
  3. 200+ integrations (Meta, Google, TikTok, affiliate networks, CRM/e-commerce)
  4. Unified Ads Manager — manage campaigns across multiple traffic sources from one dashboard
  5. Conversion API (CAPI) — feeds enriched conversion data back to ad platforms
  6. AI Co-Pilot (Beta) — report building, insight surfacing, troubleshooting
  7. Multi-channel attribution and single source of truth for clicks/conversions/revenue
  8. Real-time granular reporting and analytics
  9. Custom tracking domains and link management
  10. 14-day free trial (no credit card required)
- **Known Weaknesses:**
  - Steep learning curve — complex, technical setup not beginner-friendly
  - Customer support responsiveness issues, especially during onboarding
  - Premium pricing may be prohibitive for small-budget affiliates
  - Add-on costs for frequent API syncs and additional domains
- **Pricing Model:** Tiered by seats and volume. Annual billing offers ~2 months free.
  - **Solo:** ~$149/mo — 1 user seat, individual marketers
  - **Team:** ~$399/mo — agencies and scaling operations
  - **Enterprise:** Custom quote — high-volume agencies and large brands
- **User Traction:** One of the top 3 affiliate trackers by market share. Strong presence on G2, Trustpilot, and affiliate communities. Estimated 30K–80K active users. Used by major media buying agencies.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Conversion API (CAPI) integration that feeds enriched first-party data back to ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) — significantly improves ad algorithm optimization in post-iOS 14.5 world.
- **What users complain about:**
  - Steep learning curve and complex initial setup
  - Slow customer support response times during onboarding
  - High entry-level pricing ($149/mo) compared to BeMob ($0) or Offer18 ($49)
  - Occasional data discrepancies between RedTrack and ad platform dashboards
  - Add-on pricing for API sync frequency and extra domains
